Hot Potato: Food Waste and Carbon Emissions

This is a project I developed while thinking about current environmental and sustainability issues. Food waste particularly is one issue that I hadn’t known much about prior to a few years ago. However, as I’ve illustrated in this map story, it is an issue that's currently plaguing our world. Even worse, it’s an issue that many don’t take time to consider in their daily lives. Predominantly, I wanted to make this story to bring awareness to food waste and its prevalent issues and how it combines with the current climate crisis. Food waste and carbon emissions are two issues that often walk hand-in-hand. One thing I really like about this map is the theme!
