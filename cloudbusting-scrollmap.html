Cloudbusting

Sophie Michalski

December 2022

This map was a dream for me to make. I’ve always wanted to make a map using pink and purple colors primarily, but they often don’t make sense within a map theme. When considering the cover art for Hounds of Love, and the mood I was trying to create with the map, I decided on this colorway. I’m also overjoyous about the concept and format of this map. I’m always looking for ways to meld my personal interests with my academic career, and this map is a quintessential example. To view the high resolution pdf file, please click here.

The right side of my project details the meaning behind the map.

I drew inspiration from the colors and textures of the water, land, and other features from the film.

In the map, black cats, meant to emulate Kiki's cat, represent cities.

Other icons represent different places as well. Seagulls represent beaches, bread loaves represent cafes and bakeries, and red bows represent shops.

The map shows different amounts of detail at different zoom levels.
