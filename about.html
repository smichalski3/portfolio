Hi! I’m Sophie, and I’m a recent graduate from UW-Madison. I like reading, meandering around outside for long, long walks, admiring artwork, and traveling. I’ve recently visited both Canada and Italy, home to some of my favorite art museums and exhibits! A profound appreciation and respect for the natural world has led me into the field of Geography, particularly in the sub-disciplines that focus on environmental well-being and climate change. My interest in Cartography blossomed from my passion for graphic design in high school, so I enjoy making maps that combine my enthusiasm for art and the environment into a visual realization of the world.
