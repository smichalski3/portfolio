Hello world! I'm Sophie, a recent Cartography & GIS M.S. graduate from UW-Madison. I also currently work as a GIS Associate for OneEnergy, a solar development company. My interest in cartography blossomed from my passion for graphic design in high school, so I often enjoy making maps that combine my enthusiasm for the arts and the environmental into visual representations of the world. A profound appreciation and respect for the natural world has led me into the field of environmental and ecological conservation, but I have so many academic interests that synthesize GIS, including agrivoltaics and dual-use solar, renewable energy, art history, and graphic design. In my free time, you can find me reading, meandering around outside for very long walks, snorkeling, admiring artwork, attending musicals/plays/theatrical events and traveling all around the globe!
